The structure

Guaranteed deliverables.
Prorated if we miss them.

Reign isn't a vague retainer. Every month has defined deliverables: a content calendar delivered, a minimum number of sponsor outreach contacts made, a performance report issued.

If we miss a monthly deliverable, it's prorated from your fee. The invoice drops. That's how we stay accountable without asking you to trust us on faith.

The engagement is a 6-month minimum with an exit clause after month 3 on 30 days notice. We're confident enough in the results that we'd rather give you the out than have you locked in somewhere you don't want to be.
